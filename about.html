About

Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about some of our most precious yet endangered animal species. I strongly feel that it is our duty and responsibility as humans to make sure that we don't lose these creatures who play an important role to maintain the balance and beauty of our ecosystem and our planet, as a whole.

Vision

To inspire each and everyone of us to cooperate in this movement to create a world where there's assurance of the preservation of the animals considered to be endangered.
